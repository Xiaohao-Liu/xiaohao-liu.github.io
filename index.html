Bio
Xiaohao Liu is currently pursuing a Master's degree in cryptography at SKLIOS Lab, Chinese Academy of Science from 2021, advised by Prof. Xianhui Lu. He received a B.S. degree in computer science from the Communication University of China in 2021. His research interests include machine learning, information retrieval and information security.
